Ginzan Onsen Town

Ginzan Onsen, a hot spring town with a 500-year history, is home to the popular Notoya bathhouse, which served as the inspiration for Hayao Miyazaki's "Spirited Away." Renowned as a therapeutic hot spring during the Edo period, its streets and alleys still retain their original charm. Along the tranquil banks of the Ginzan River, wooden inns built between the Taisho and early Showa periods of Japan stand side by side.

Naruko Onsen-Kameya Onsen Hotel

Naruko Onsen
is a hot spring resort located in a mountain village in Osaki City, Miyagi Prefecture. In the steaming hot spring town, there are plenty of hot spring inns, foot baths, and day-trip hot springs, so you can easily enjoy the hot springs. In addition, there are workshops making traditional crafts called "Naruko Kikukashi" and long-established restaurants,

Zao Ice Trees

The dry Siberian monsoon winds meet the Sea of Japan, becoming moistened winds. These winds then collide with and solidify the Aomori pine trees growing near the summit, creating this work of art from nature. This spectacle only appears in Yamagata or Aomori prefectures in the Tohoku region of Japan. The unique climate and vegetation of the Zao mountain range have created countless artistic shapes, resembling snow monsters; hence, the ice trees are also known as "Ice Monsters." Zao is the only place in Japan where you can appreciate these enormous and beautiful ice trees, making it internationally renowned.

Zao Fox Village

Located in the mountains of Baekseok City, this fox park houses over 100 foxes of seven different species, including Arctic foxes, cross foxes, and swarm foxes. Visitors can feed and photograph the free-roaming foxes, and even hold a fox cub.

Onihira Edo- do Site

Onihira Edo Area is a roadside rest area themed around the Edo period, inspired by the world of "Onihira Hankacho . " Opened in 2013, it's based on the period novel "Onihira Hankacho" by Shotaro Ikenami. Designed by historical experts and an art director, it boasts a rich historical atmosphere and cultural ambiance .
